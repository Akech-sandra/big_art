About Us

BigArtUg promotes and preserves Uganda's cultural heritage through artistic expression. We showcase local artists' creativity in painting, sculpture, and ceramics, fostering a vibrant art community. Our mission is to celebrate diversity and inspire creativity in every piece we exhibit.
